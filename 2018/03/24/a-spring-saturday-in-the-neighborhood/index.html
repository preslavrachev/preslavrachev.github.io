24 March 2018 / Munich

A Spring Saturday in the Neighborhood

The winter has reigned in Munich far too long this year. Thankfully, the first signs of the coming spring are here. A couple of days ago, the spring season officially started, and with that, the first warming sun rays began popping up. There was no better time than this morning, to go out, get a bitter double espresso, and take a few video shots around the neighborhood. It has been almost half a year since my last video, so my iMovie skills might have became a little rusty (if one could ever say that for iMovie), but I hope you still enjoy the short video.
